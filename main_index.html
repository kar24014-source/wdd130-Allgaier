An Experience

Every time you walk into a bakery or restaurant, it’s a feast for all of your senses. Your eyes bounce back the light of a hundred brightly colored treats; your ears are met with hustle and bustle or cozy and calm; your nose is greeted with the warm, sweet, buttery luxury of freshly baked goods.

Inspiration

Our bakery is inspired by countless experiences and memories, from treasured family recipes like grandma’s orange rolls to trips to Europe indulging in pain au chocolat. We believe that everyone should get to enjoy a delicious treat, and we offer nut-free, gluten-free, and refined sugar-free options.
